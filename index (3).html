Who We Are

Sasaram's Premium Fitness Destination

R.D Fitness A Gym was built for one purpose: give Sasaram a training floor that matches the ambition of the people who walk in every morning. From free weights to functional zones, every corner is designed around real results.

Our mission is simple — certified coaching, modern machines, and a motivating atmosphere where beginners and competitive lifters train side by side. Members consistently highlight the energy of the floor as much as the equipment on it.

Common Questions

What are your gym timings?+

We're open 5:00 AM – 10:00 PM, Monday through Saturday. We're closed on Sundays.

Do you offer a free trial?+

Yes — book a free trial session through the contact form or by calling us directly, and a trainer will walk you through the floor.

Do you provide personal training?+

Yes, one-on-one personal training is included from the Standard plan upward, with fully custom programming on Premium.

Is there a women's fitness program?+

Yes, we run a dedicated women's fitness track with trainers focused on strength, tone and confidence-building.
